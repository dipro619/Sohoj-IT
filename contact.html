Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What are your course admission requirements?

Most of our courses require basic computer knowledge. For advanced courses like Web Development, we recommend having basic understanding of HTML/CSS. No formal education background is required for most courses.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

Yes! We have a dedicated placement cell that helps our students with job placements, internships, and freelancing opportunities. We also conduct regular job fairs and have partnerships with IT companies.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfer, bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and credit/debit cards. We also offer installment plans for most courses to make them more accessible.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer both online and offline classes. You can choose the mode that suits you best. Our online classes are conducted through Zoom/Google Meet with live instructor support.
